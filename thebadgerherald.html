Wahl's lifelong dream evolves into five-year reality at Wisconsin

This article details Tyler Wahl's basketball career as he prepares to finish his fifth season at the University of Wisconsin. He was an absolute joy to speak with and was friendly and welcoming. His high school coach, John Oxton, was the same way. I loved speaking with them, and tried to tell the story of Wahl so that he, his family, as well as Oxton and Lakeville North can be celebrated.
